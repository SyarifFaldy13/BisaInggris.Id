LEVEL A1 – BEGINNER

UNTUK PEMULA MUTLAK

Bagi yang belum pernah belajar bahasa Inggris atau hanya mengenal sedikit kosakata

GRAMMAR

  • Subject pronouns
  • To be (am/is/are)
  • Simple present tense
  • Present continuous
  • Articles (a/an/the)
  • Basic prepositions (in, on, at, under)
  • Plural nouns
  • This/that/these/those
  • Can / can’t
  • Yes/No & Wh- questions

VOCABULARY THEMES

  • Greetings & introductions
  • Family members
  • School objects & classroom language
  • Numbers, days, months, time
  • Colors, shapes, clothes
  • Food & drinks
  • Hobbies & daily routines
  • Places in town

4 CORE SKILLS

Listening: Short dialogues (name, age, likes)
Speaking: Self-introduction, describe family/routine
Reading: ID cards, timetables, simple emails
Writing: Fill-in forms, 3–5 simple sentences about self

LEVEL A2 – ELEMENTARY

UNTUK PEMULA LANJUTAN

Bagi yang sudah bisa berkomunikasi dasar namun ingin mengembangkan kemampuan lebih jauh

GRAMMAR

  • Simple past (regular & common irregular verbs)
  • Past continuous
  • Future: “going to” & “will”
  • Adverbs of frequency
  • Comparatives & superlatives (short adjectives)
  • There is / There are
  • Possessive adjectives & pronouns
  • Modal verbs: should, must, have to
  • Countable/uncountable nouns + some/any/much/many

VOCABULARY THEMES

  • Daily routines & weekend activities
  • Travel & transportation
  • Weather & seasons
  • Jobs & professions
  • Health & body parts
  • Shopping & prices
  • Basic technology
  • Describing people & personalities

4 CORE SKILLS

Listening: Short stories, announcements
Speaking: Talk about past weekend, give simple advice
Reading: Recount texts, ads, schedules
Writing: Diary entry, short email, paragraph about past experience
Private: Rp25.000/sesi | Small Group: Rp100.000/sesi

LEVEL B1 – INTERMEDIATE

UNTUK MENENGAH

Bagi yang sudah cukup lancar berbicara dan ingin menguasai struktur tata bahasa kompleks

GRAMMAR

  • Present perfect vs. past simple
  • Past perfect (intro)
  • Future continuous & future perfect (basic)
  • Passive voice (present & past)
  • Reported speech (basic)
  • Zero, First, Second conditionals
  • Relative clauses (who/which/that)
  • Common phrasal verbs
  • Gerund vs. infinitive

VOCABULARY THEMES

  • Education & future plans
  • Environment & pollution
  • Social media & digital life
  • Culture & traditions
  • Emotions & opinions
  • Crime & safety
  • Global issues (basic)
  • Work & career aspirations

4 CORE SKILLS

Listening: Podcasts, news clips, interviews
Speaking: Express opinions, agree/disagree with reasons
Reading: News articles, blog posts, short essays
Writing: Opinion paragraph, formal/informal email, descriptive/recount text, short report
Private: Rp25.000/sesi | Small Group: Rp100.000/sesi
12 pertemuan, 90 menit/sesi
LEVEL B2 – UPPER-INTERMEDIATE

UNTUK MENENGAH LANJUT

Bagi yang ingin mencapai kemahiran akademik atau profesional dalam bahasa Inggris

GRAMMAR

  • All perfect tenses
  • Passive voice in all tenses
  • Third & Mixed conditionals
  • Inversion (e.g., Not only did he…)
  • Cleft sentences
  • Advanced modals (might have, should have)
  • Subjunctive (basic)
  • Discourse markers (however, furthermore, etc.)

VOCABULARY THEMES

  • Academic & formal vocabulary
  • Politics & society
  • Science & tech trends
  • Literature & film
  • Ethics & philosophy (basic)
  • Business & economics (intro)
  • Idioms & collocations

4 CORE SKILLS

Listening: TED Talks, documentaries, debates
Speaking: Debating, presenting arguments, summarizing
Reading: Editorials, research abstracts, literary excerpts
Writing: Argumentative essay, summary, analytical exposition, formal letter

TENTANG KAMI

Pengajar ahli dengan latar belakang akademik terbaik

Les privat BIsaInggris.Id dipandu oleh pengajar yang merupakan Lulusan Terbaik Jurusan Pendidikan Bahasa Inggris dari IKIP-PGRI Pontianak dengan predikat cum laude.

Selain itu, pengajar juga telah lulus program PPG Calon Guru G2 2024 dari Universitas Tanjungpura, sehingga memiliki sertifikasi resmi sebagai guru bahasa Inggris profesional yang diakui oleh Kementerian Pendidikan, Kebudayaan, Riset, dan Teknologi RI.

Dengan kombinasi kompetensi akademik, sertifikasi resmi, dan pengalaman mengajar siswa SMP–SMA di Sambas, kami siap membimbing Anda mencapai target belajar bahasa Inggris secara efektif, menyenangkan, dan berbasis kurikulum internasional CEFR.
